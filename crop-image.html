How to Use Image Cropper

1
Select an Image
Click the image selection box to choose an image from your device.
2
Choose Aspect Ratio
Select from various aspect ratio options or use "Freeform" for custom cropping.
3
Adjust Crop Area
Drag the corners or edges of the crop box to adjust the cropping area.
4
Rotate if Needed
Use the rotate button to adjust the image orientation.
5
Download Your Image
Choose your preferred format (PNG or JPG) and click "Download Cropped Image".
Pro Tip

Use the Freeform option for custom cropping. You can also rotate the image for a perfect fit.
