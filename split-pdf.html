How to Use Split PDF Tool

1
Upload your PDF
Drag and drop your PDF file into the designated area or click to select a file from your computer.
2
Select pages
Click on the page thumbnails you want to include in your new PDF. Selected pages will be highlighted.
3
Download
Click the "Download Selected Pages" button to get your customized PDF containing only the selected pages.
Pro Tip

All processing happens locally in your browser - your files never leave your computer.
